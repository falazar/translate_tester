FRENCH 101 - WEEK BY WEEK CONTENT PLAN
======================================
16 Weeks Total | 5 Days per Week | 1 Hour per Day
Target: A2 CEFR Level | DELF A2 Preparation

SEMESTER CHAPTER OVERVIEW:
Week 1: Chapitre Préliminaire (Présentons-nous!) + Chapitre 1 (Ma famille et moi) - first half
Week 2: Chapitre 1 (Ma famille et moi) - second half + Chapitre 2 (Voici mes amis) - first half
Week 3: Chapitre 2 (Voici mes amis) - second half + Chapitre 3 (Études et professions) - first half
Week 4: Chapitre 3 (Études et professions) - second half + ORAL 1
Week 5: Chapitre 4 (Métro, boulot, dodo) - complete
Week 6: Chapitre 5 (Du marché à la table) - complete
Week 7: Chapitre 6 (Nous sommes chez nous) - first half (passé composé intro)
Week 8: MIDTERM (Review Chapitres Préliminaire through 6)
Week 9: BREAK WEEK (light review, catch-up)
Week 10: Chapitre 6 (Nous sommes chez nous) - second half (passé composé mastery)
Week 11: Chapitre 7 (Les relations personnelles) - first half (imparfait intro) + ORAL 2
Week 12: Chapitre 7 (Les relations personnelles) - second half + ORAL 3
Week 13: Chapitre 8 (Activités par tous les temps) - complete
Week 14: Chapitre 9 (Voyageons!) + DELF A2 Practice Tests + ORAL 4
Week 15: Review + Selected Chapitre 10 topics (La santé et le bien-être)
Week 16: FINAL EXAM WEEK

======================================
WEEK 1: Greetings, Introductions & French Basics
======================================
Textbook: Chapitre Préliminaire - Présentons-nous! (pp. 2-27)
Homework: Textbook pp. 2-9 reading, practice dialogues, alphabet practice, journal entry, flashcards
Grammar Focus: Subject pronouns (je, tu, vous), present tense of s'appeler and être
Vocabulary Theme: Greetings (formal/informal), introductions, classroom vocabulary, numbers 0-100, alphabet
Cultural Topic: Tu vs. vous distinction, French greetings (handshake, la bise), Francophone world overview

Day 1 (Monday): Diagnostic test (20 min) | Course introduction & syllabus (20 min) | First French lesson: greetings and Je m'appelle (20 min) | Textbook pp. 3-4

Day 2 (Tuesday): Warm-up greetings practice (10 min) | Tu vs. Vous lesson (15 min) | Basic greeting responses (15 min) | French alphabet & pronunciation (15 min) | Closing (5 min) | Textbook pp. 4-9

Day 3 (Wednesday): Numbers 0-30, classroom objects, classroom commands | James looking for apartment

Day 4 (Thursday): Numbers 31-100, days of week, telling time | Textbook exercises review

Day 5 (Friday): Week 1 review, cultural topics, conversation practice | QUIZ 1 (last 15 min) - greetings, tu/vous, alphabet, numbers 0-30

======================================
WEEK 2: Family & Descriptions / Friends & Activities
======================================
Textbook: Chapitre 1 (Ma famille et moi) - second half + Chapitre 2 (Voici mes amis) - first half
Homework: Family descriptions, create family tree, -er verb conjugation practice, textbook exercises Ch 1-2
Grammar Focus: Verbs être and avoir (all forms), -er verb conjugations (present tense), adjective agreement
Vocabulary Theme: Family members, physical descriptions, personality traits, leisure activities
Cultural Topic: French family structure, naming conventions, popular French leisure activities

Day 1 (Monday): Review Week 1 quiz (10 min) | Verb être - full conjugation (15 min) | Family vocabulary (20 min) | James describes his family (10 min) | Textbook Ch. 1 pp. 28-35

Day 2 (Tuesday): Verb avoir - full conjugation (15 min) | Possessive adjectives (mon, ma, mes, etc.) (20 min) | Describing people - physical traits (15 min) | Activity: Describe your family member (10 min) | Textbook Ch. 1 pp. 36-42

Day 3 (Wednesday): Adjective agreement - masculine/feminine/plural (20 min) | Personality adjectives (15 min) | Cultural topic: French families (10 min) | Pair activity: Family interviews (15 min) | Textbook Ch. 1 pp. 43-50

Day 4 (Thursday): Introduction to -er verbs (parler, aimer, étudier) (20 min) | Conjugation patterns (15 min) | Leisure activities vocabulary (15 min) | James meets friends Pierre and Sophie (10 min) | Textbook Ch. 2 pp. 51-58

Day 5 (Friday): Practice -er verbs with activities (15 min) | Conversation: What do you like to do? (20 min) | Review game: Family & activities (10 min) | QUIZ 2 (15 min) - être/avoir, family vocab, basic -er verbs

======================================
WEEK 3: Activities & Hobbies / Studies & Professions
======================================
Textbook: Chapitre 2 (Voici mes amis) - second half + Chapitre 3 (Études et professions) - first half
Homework: Activity preferences writing, school schedule creation, -ir/-re verb exercises, textbook Ch 2-3
Grammar Focus: Negation (ne...pas), question formation (est-ce que, inversion), -ir and -re verb conjugations
Vocabulary Theme: Sports, hobbies, music, school subjects, professions, workplace vocabulary
Cultural Topic: French education system, popular French sports, work-life balance in France

Day 1 (Monday): Review Quiz 2 (10 min) | More -er verbs and preferences (aimer/adorer/détester) (20 min) | Sports and hobbies vocabulary (15 min) | Activity: Survey classmates' hobbies (15 min) | Textbook Ch. 2 pp. 59-68

Day 2 (Tuesday): Negation: ne...pas (20 min) | Asking questions: est-ce que vs. inversion (20 min) | Practice conversations with questions (15 min) | James discusses what he likes/dislikes (5 min) | Textbook Ch. 2 pp. 69-78

Day 3 (Wednesday): School subjects vocabulary (15 min) | Talking about classes and schedules (20 min) | Cultural: French education system (lycée, université) (10 min) | Create your ideal schedule (15 min) | Textbook Ch. 3 pp. 79-88

Day 4 (Thursday): Professions vocabulary (20 min) | Introduction to -ir verbs (finir, choisir, réussir) (20 min) | Introduction to -re verbs (vendre, attendre, répondre) (15 min) | James considers his career (5 min) | Textbook Ch. 3 pp. 89-96

Day 5 (Friday): Review -er, -ir, -re verb patterns (15 min) | Conversation practice: studies and future plans (20 min) | Review game: All verb types (10 min) | QUIZ 3 (15 min) - negation, questions, school vocab, intro -ir/-re verbs
