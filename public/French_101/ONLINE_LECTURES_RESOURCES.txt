ONLINE FRENCH LECTURES & RESOURCES
===================================
Free University-Level French Courses Available Online
Updated: January 2026

===================================
COMPLETE UNIVERSITY COURSES (FREE)
===================================

1. MIT OpenCourseWare - French I & II
   URL: ocw.mit.edu/courses/french-i-through-iv/
   - Complete course materials from MIT
   - Lecture notes, assignments, exams
   - No video lectures but excellent written materials
   - Syllabus and lesson plans available
   - FREE

2. Carnegie Mellon University - Open Learning Initiative
   URL: oli.cmu.edu/courses/french-i/
   - Interactive online French course
   - Self-paced modules
   - Grammar exercises
   - Some multimedia content
   - FREE

3. France Université Numérique (FUN-MOOC)
   URL: fun-mooc.fr
   Courses Available:
   - "Vivre en France" (A2 level)
   - "Pratiques du français" (B1-B2)
   - Video lectures by French professors
   - Completely in French
   - FREE with optional certificate

4. Coursera - University of Paris Courses
   URL: coursera.org
   - "Étudier en France: French Intermediate course B1-B2"
   - Video lectures from Sorbonne professors
   - Some courses FREE to audit
   - Paid certificate option

5. EdX - Various Universities
   URL: edx.org
   Courses:
   - "AP French Language and Culture" (CollegeBoard)
   - Various French courses from international universities
   - FREE to audit
   - Video lectures included

===================================
WHY WE'RE MOVING AWAY FROM FRENCH IN ACTION
===================================

French in Action Issues:
- Filmed in 1987 (nearly 40 years old)
- Outdated cultural references
- 1980s fashion, technology, social norms
- Slower pacing than modern learners expect
- Not relatable for Gen Z students

Better Modern Alternatives:
✓ Easy French (2013-present): Current, authentic, engaging
✓ Learn French with Alexa (2011-present): Professional, complete courses
✓ Various MOOC platforms: Contemporary university content

Our Approach:
- Use Alexa for structure and grammar (replaces textbook lectures)
- Use Easy French for authentic immersion (replaces French in Action)
- Much more engaging for 2026 students
